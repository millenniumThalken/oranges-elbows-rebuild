Our Services

Oranges & Elbows will refresh your home from floor to ceiling with all our standard cleaning practices, which includes using only all-natural, non-toxic products. We make our most frequently used products right at home, adding essential oils for their antiviral and antibacterial properties. With the right tools, the best products, and a little elbow grease we can clean just about anything!



Below are our standard cleaning practices for every room in your home or business. Have something else in mind that you don’t see listed? Just ask! Oranges & Elbows creates a custom cleaning plan with each client; our standard services are only a starting point for what’s possible. Together, we’ll create the plan and schedule—weekly, semi-monthly, monthly, or one-time—to suit your needs. Schedule an estimate today to get started!

Below are our standard cleaning practices for every room in your home or business.

Standard Services:

Dusting
Are cobwebs filling corners in hard to reach places? Is your bathroom vent full of dust bunnies? Do your bookshelves store drifts of dust? Oranges & Elbows has you covered with floor-to-ceiling dusting. During your initial cleaning, we will take down that built-up dust and each subsequent cleaning we will do the work to maintain those ceiling fans, walls, tops of fridges, and more.
Floors & Surfaces
Tile, carpet, hardwood, glass, cement, marble, laminate—you name it, Oranges & Elbows can clean it. We have all the right all-natural cleaning products to clean and preserve your floors (even pesky grout lines!) Surfaces like countertops, backsplashes, cupboards, vanities, and mirrors will all receive a through cleaning and polish.
Appliances & Fixtures
Stainless steel, chrome, glass, and plastics come together on modern appliances, and Oranges & Elbows can tackle whatever food, fingerprints, and water marks collect on these materials. Water faucets and shower heads will be shined up. Toilet rings will disappear along with the germs after we scrub and sanitize them, too.

A standard Oranges & Elbows visit won’t include inside the refrigerator, microwave, coffee pot, toaster, oven, or any other appliance, unless you ask for it specifically.
Upholstery & Rugs
It may be raining cats, dogs, and children at your house, but we are happy to vacuum away all the hair their downpour leaves behind. Oranges & Elbows can keep these woven surfaces tidy, using attachments to carefully handle delicate rugs and fringe trims while still whisking away the hair and fur.

What we don't do

Oranges & Elbows does not provide the following services as part of our cleanings:

  • Window washing
  • Dishes
  • Laundry
  • Organizing
  • Pet messes (bathroom accidents, litter boxes, etc.)

What to Expect

If you’ve never had a cleaning service before or even if you are looking to switch to a new one, you may be wondering what to expect. Here’s the Oranges & Elbows process:

Call or fill out our online form to schedule a free estimate
Estimates are a free consultation where we will evaluate your cleaning goals and the size of your home or business to provide you with an estimate of the time and cost of our services for your particular needs. During the estimate, you can discuss with our most experienced staff members any special cleaning needs or concerns you have so that you are entirely comfortable before our initial cleaning takes place.
The initial
During the initial cleaning, Oranges & Elbows will dive into all the nooks and crannies of your home. This deep cleaning allows us to reach your cleaning goals and sets us up to perform more routine cleanings on the frequency schedule of your choice. The initial may include steps like taking down light fixtures to thoroughly dust or wash them and scrubbing scuff marks off of baseboards. After we tackle these big projects once, we can keep on top of them with faster, regular cleanings.
Become a regular
After your initial cleaning, you can become a regular! This means getting a spot on our schedule, whether it’s once or twice a month or every week. We can help you decide what frequency fits your needs and budget. Oranges & Elbows also loves getting feedback from our clients, especially after the initial cleaning. Anything we missed? Something extra you would like done? You will receive impeccable customer service to make sure each and every cleaning is exactly what you want.
